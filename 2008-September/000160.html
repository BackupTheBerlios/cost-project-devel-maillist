Ok nice,

from what you describe, you are right to say it is A2, and to my 
knowledge, it is the most common strategy used all around. Moreover, A1 
is very close to A2 in term of coding, and if I understand well, the 
discards information you have is A1. In fact, discards will never be A2!
Concerning A3, the French sole dataset is a real example of that 
strategy, but only for market sampling. As the commercial category is 
sorted under auction, the onboard observer sampling is A1. Hope it is 
easy to follow this explanation!!

At the end, your development is in line with the others, as I'm not 
aware of any development of the B strategy. Can the other developers 
confirm or not this statement.

Best regards

Joel
