Hi David,

sorry for the delay to comment your mail.
Can you summarise what the English/Scottish data are, in term of 
Sampling strategy as defined in the minutes of the 1st Expert meeting, 
please:

A1 - Sampling for length in unsorted catches + ALK
A2 - Sampling for length in catches sorted by categories + ALK
A3 - Sampling for length in commercial categories + ALK
B1 - Sampling directly for age in unsorted catches
B2 - Sampling directly for age in catches sorted by categories
B3 - Sampling directly for age in commercial categories

Considering the structure of the Data Exchange Format, all countries 
applying the same strategy should be able to make use of your 
developments. Moreover, the difference between 2 strategies are often 
very weak and only minor adjustments are needed to extend the use of the 
functions.
