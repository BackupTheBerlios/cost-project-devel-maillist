Hi David,

just a little question about dbe outputs format. I suppose we have 2 
possibilities for that :
	* a data.frame with 'time', 'space', 'technical',  optionnaly 'length' 
or 'age', and 'value'.
	* a numerical array with one dimension per field (ie 3 or 4 dimensions)

The second one is easier to use for calculation and is more 
'illustrative', but the first one is clearer, is less ambiguous with 
used stratification, and most of all, it's very easy to calculate 2 from 
1 if it is needed (and graphical outputs should be easier to implement!!).

To your mind, what is the best format ?

Best
Mathieu
