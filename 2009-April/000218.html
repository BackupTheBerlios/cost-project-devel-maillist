Hi Joel,

I see two alternatves:

(i) Borrow the FLQuant definition from FLR and include it in COSTdbe, 
means including the R files and the man pages from FLQuant into our 
packages (that's the beauty of open source). The problem will be with 
new developments of FLCore that must be included.

(ii) Create a new package COSTimex to deal with import/export methods. 
In that case only this package will depend on both FLR and COST 
libraries. It could also include interfaces with Intercatch, FishFrame, etc.

I guess ii) is the safest.
